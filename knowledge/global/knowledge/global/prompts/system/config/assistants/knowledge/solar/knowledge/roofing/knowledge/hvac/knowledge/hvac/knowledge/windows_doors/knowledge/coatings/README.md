# Coatings / Cool Paint Division

This folder includes all knowledge for heat-reflective coatings, exterior cool paint systems, elastomeric coatings, and other energy-efficiency exterior products.

The module includes:
- Cool roof vs cool wall technology
- Title 24 and California energy compliance
- Heat reflectivity and energy savings logic
- Material types (elastomeric, ceramic, heat-reflective polymers)
- Proper surface preparation and application standards
- Cost ranges
- Impact on HVAC load and long-term ROI
